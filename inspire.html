What makes a Star?

Mike Evans was drafted as a seventh overall pick by the Tampa Bay Buccaneers in the 2014 draft.Before his draft, he earned consensus all-american honors at Texas A&M for a record 1,394 receiving yards on 69 receptions.

As the end of the 2023-2024 season comes to a close, Mike Evans has set an NFL record for having over 1,000 recieving yards for his first 10 seasons. He continues this record into the This makes him in a league of his own.He is certainly a top quality candidate for the Hall of Fame at only 30 years old. He will be a free agent next year and will be looking at recieving a hefty pay.
